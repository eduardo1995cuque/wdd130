Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to promote a sense of adventure, promote environmental responsibility, and create unforgettable experiences for outdoor enthusiasts of all ages. We offer amazing whitewater rafting adventures.

Audience

Target audience:

  • Urban Millennials
  • Early 20s to mid-30s
  • Middle- to upper-middle class income
  • Tech-savvy
  • Socially conscious
  • Single professionals for week long trips What do they want?
  • They seek unique travel experiences that are Instagram-worthy.
  • They desire immersive cultural experiences and off-the-beaten-path adventures.
  • They prioritize eco-conscious and sustainable travel options.
  • They want to connect with like-minded travelers and locals.
  • Needs to be met?
  • Access to eco-friendly accommodations and tours.
  • Opportunities for volunteering or giving back to local communities.
  • There are options for socializing and connecting with fellow travelers through forums or meetups.

Our target demographic comprises mostly of young to middle-aged people who are naturally adventurous and seek exhilarating experiences to break up the monotony of everyday life. They like adventure, outdoor thrills, and uncovering nature's riches.
