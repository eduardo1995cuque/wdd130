Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to promote a sense of adventure, promote environmental responsibility, and create unforgettable experiences for outdoor enthusiasts of all ages. We offer amazing whitewater rafting adventures.

Audience

Target audience:

  • Adventurous
  • Mid 20's to 50
  • Upper middle class to upper class income
  • healthy lifestyle
  • family oriented for one and two day trips
  • Single professionals for week long trips What do they want?
  • These people want a fun adventure to share with family or friends/co-workers
  • They want to experience something new to them
  • They want to re-experience the adrenaline rush you get while rafting. Needs to being met?
  • By outdoor supplies relating to water activities
  • Want a fun and safe way to satisfy an adrenalin rush What devices will be use to access the site?

Our target demographic comprises mostly of young to middle-aged people who are naturally adventurous and seek exhilarating experiences to break up the monotony of everyday life. They like adventure, outdoor thrills, and uncovering nature's riches.
